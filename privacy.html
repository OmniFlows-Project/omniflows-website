Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 20, 2026

OmniFlows ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the OmniFlows browser extension.

1. Information We Collect

To provide its core functionality, OmniFlows handles the following types of data:

  • Website Content: Text snippets and images that you explicitly choose to "capture" or highlight.
  • Web History: The URL and page title of the websites where you perform captures, to provide context and source attribution.
  • User Activity: Minimal engagement metrics (such as dwell time and scroll depth) to power the "Daily Review" and learning analysis features.

2. How We Use Information

The data collected is used solely for:

  • Allowing you to curate and organize your personal research.
  • Synchronizing your data to your authorized third-party integrations (Notion, Obsidian, Google Drive).
  • Generating personal AI-driven insights and summaries for your own review.

3. Data Storage and Third-Party Integrations

Your data is yours. OmniFlows does not store your captured research on our own servers. Data is stored locally in your browser and transmitted directly to the third-party services you explicitly connect (Notion, Obsidian, or Google Drive) via their official APIs.

Please review the privacy policies of these third-party services before connecting them.

4. No Data Selling

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to outside parties. Your information is never used for advertising or profiling.

5. Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data during transmission and keep your authentication tokens secure within your browser's local storage.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@omniflows.net
